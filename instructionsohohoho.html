Instructions for the exam

  • Registered teams may begin the contest any time during the six-hour contest window.
  • On the contest website, the answers to the problems and the names of the winning teams will be announced.
  • CHEATING from another teams isn’t allowed.
  • You can use Graphical calculators, programming, Wolframalpha, etc to solve some problems.
  • The contest consists of 20 problems, where the points are gradually distributed among the questions according to their difficulty.
  • You have 135 min to solve the 20 problems with 135 points. TAKE CARE OF YOU TIME TO AVOID DISQUALIFICATION.
  • ONLLLLYYYYY ONE MEMBER OF THE TEAM SHOULD SUBMIT THE ANSWERS.
  • Results will be available after the last team submitting their answer.
